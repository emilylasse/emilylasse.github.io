Cancer Biology PhD Candidate

About me

I am a 5th year Cancer Biology PhD Candidate in the Pasca di Magliano Lab at the University of Michigan. My research focuses on premetastatic niche formation during pancreatic cancer progression using mouse modeling and large single cell RNA sequencing datasets.

Currently looking for a post-doctoral fellowship where I can continue to hone my bioinformatics skills.
